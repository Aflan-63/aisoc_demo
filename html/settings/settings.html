Password Policy

We are very strict about our password policy. Read through our policy to maintain our password policy to continuously use our services without interruption.
  • 1. Password MUST be changed in every 3 months.
  • 2. Password MUST contain at least one number.
  • 3. Password MUST contain one uppercase and one lowercase number.
  • 4. Password MUST contain one special character (@, $, %, ^, !).
